Determining the Extinguishing Status of Fuel Flames With Sound Wave by Machine Learning Methods

About

Fire is a natural calamity that can occur for a variety of reasons. Traditional fire-extinguishing methods may produce chemical waste, which is hazardous to human health. Research on fire-extinguishing with renewable energy sources has been conducted in attempt to remove these effects.
A sound wave fire-extinguishing system was developed as part of these revolutionary extinguishing approaches, and firefighting experiments were also conducted.
